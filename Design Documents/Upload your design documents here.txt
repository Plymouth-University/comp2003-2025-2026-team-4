Plan - Implementing drag & drop 

To have functional drag and drop editing, following functions should exist for the user: 

-move element 

-resize element 

-add new element 

-upload image 

-delete 

-edit text boxes 

-admin login 

-save/undo/redo changes 

-enter editing mode 

-select element
