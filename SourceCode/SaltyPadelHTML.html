Who We Are

Salty Padel is a growing padel community bringing competitive and social tournaments to the South West. Our events are designed to connect players, create great experiences on court, and help grow the game across Devon and Cornwall.

Alongside organising events, Salty Padel champions all things padel — from helping players discover the sport to providing equipment such as rackets and grips. Whether it’s a friendly Americano, a larger mixed tournament, or helping someone find the right gear, the goal is always the same: to grow the game and build a welcoming community where players of all levels feel included.

From grassroots players trying padel for the first time to experienced competitors, Salty Padel is about bringing people together through the sport and creating a positive, energetic atmosphere on and off the court.

My Story

Hey there! I’m Philip Salter — tournament organiser, community builder, and the driving force behind Salty Padel.

I first fell in love with padel when my local facility opened in October 2024 at City Padel Exeter. Having not really come from a racket sports background, it was the perfect sport to get into. What immediately stood out to me was how inclusive and social the game is — players of all levels mixing, having fun, and building a real community.

Since Spring 2025, I’ve organised tournaments ranging from intimate 24-player events to large 96-player mixed competitions, all designed to grow the game and strengthen the padel community across Devon and Cornwall.

From scheduling matches and designing formats to making sure every player feels welcome, I’m passionate about creating high-quality events with smooth organisation, great energy, and a strong community spirit.

Our Vision

Our vision is to continue growing the game of padel while keeping it as inclusive and accessible as possible. We aim to expand into more regions across the South West, bringing players together through well-run events and a welcoming community.

At the same time, we want Salty Padel tournaments to offer fantastic prizes, rewards, and experiences that make every event feel special — whether you’re playing your first competition or competing for the podium.

What We Do

At Salty Padel, everything we do is built around one simple goal

Growing the game while creating a strong, welcoming community for players across the South West.

Tournaments & Events

We organise a range of padel events designed for all levels — from relaxed, social Americanos to larger competitive tournaments. Every event is carefully planned to deliver a smooth, high-quality experience, with great formats, fair play, and an energetic atmosphere on and off the court.

Whether you’re stepping into your first competition or pushing for the podium, our events are designed to be inclusive, well-organised, and genuinely enjoyable.

Building the Community

Salty Padel is more than just tournaments — it’s a growing network of players across Devon and Cornwall.

We actively support the community by:

  • Helping players connect with others at a similar level
  • Giving players a space to share and organise matches
  • Encouraging new players to get involved in the sport
  • Creating a friendly, social environment where everyone feels welcome

If you’re looking for a game, our community makes it easy to find playing partners and stay active.

Equipment & Advice

Getting started in padel — or improving your game — shouldn’t be complicated.

We help players by:

  • Offering guidance on choosing the right rackets and grips
  • Providing honest, practical advice based on playing experience
  • Supporting players who are new to the sport and unsure where to begin

Whether you’re buying your first racket or upgrading your setup, we’re here to help you make the right choice.

Growing the Game

We’re passionate about introducing more people to padel and helping the sport thrive in the South West.

Through our events, community, and support, we:

  • Make padel accessible to beginners
  • Create opportunities for players to compete and improve
  • Build connections between clubs and players across the region

Partners

We’re proud to work with partners who help elevate the Salty Padel experience for our community.

Padel Vibes support our tournaments by supplying high-quality overgrips, helping players perform at their best on court. Their grips are a favourite within the community — reliable, comfortable, and built for competitive play.

Through our partnership with Express Padel, we’re also able to offer the community access to great deals on padel equipment. From rackets to accessories, players can benefit from competitive pricing and free next-day delivery, making it easier than ever to get the gear you need.

The Salty Padel Experience

More than a tournament — it’s the Salty Padel experience.

Everything we do is centred around creating a positive, inclusive, and well-run experience.

From your first hit to your next tournament win, Salty Padel is here to support your journey — helping you play more, meet more people, and enjoy the game to the fullest.
